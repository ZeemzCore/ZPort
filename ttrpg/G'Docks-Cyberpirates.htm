G'Docks

A Cyberpirate Adventure

Welcome to G'Docks

G'Docks is a notorious freeport, a sprawling digital haven for cyber pirates, smugglers, and all those who thrive in the shadowy corners of the data streams. Here, in this chaotic port, your crew will find both danger and opportunity in equal measure. Your ship has arrived, after a long haul, and the digital sea is yours for the taking.

Adventure Highlights

The Port Authority

G'Docks is ostensibly lawless, but a shadowy Port Authority, rumored to be controlled by a powerful AI, keeps a loose grip on things. Landing fees, docking protocols, and unspoken rules govern interactions. How will you deal with them?

Black Market Auction

A rare piece of black market cyberware is up for grabs, attracting the attention of rival pirate crews and corporate agents. Will you navigate the cutthroat auction, or resort to more... direct methods?

The Informant

A mysterious informant offers a lucrative but dangerous job. Their motives are unclear, and trust is a rare commodity in G'Docks. What secrets do they hold, and what are the risks involved?

Your Adventure Awaits

This is just a taste of what awaits you in G'Docks. Your crew will face tough decisions, navigate treacherous alliances, and roll the dice to determine your fate. Will you rise to become legends of the digital sea, or will you become another forgotten tale in the port's dark underbelly? The choice is yours.
